Hillel's steakhouse is located adjacent to the wave resort in long branch NJ, An upscale trendy steakhouse and lounge Designed by HH designers with a trendy bar, a wood-fired oven, and a meat aging fridge. Serving a full menu ranging from Fish appetizers, Salads, Woodfired flatbreads, and of course STEAKS!

In addition to the dining out, We will be offering Shabbot meals to the locals as well as vacationers staying at one of the area hotels.

Hillel's steakhouse will be under the strict supervision of the JSOR, All our Meat and Chicken products are “Alle” or “Solomon’s” All Our Vegetables are washed and checked by a JSOR certified full time Mashgiach. Bishul Bet Yosef and Bet Yosef meat available upon request. Yoshon and Pat Yisroel upon request.
